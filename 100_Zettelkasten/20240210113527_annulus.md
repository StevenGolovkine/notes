# Annulus

An annulus is the collection of points on and between two concentric circles.

$$\mathcal{A}_{a, b} = \{(x, y) \in \mathbb{R}, x^2 + y^2 \geq a, x^2 + y^2 \leq b\}, \quad a < b.$$
