# Closure

Let $(X, \mathfrak{S})$ be a topological space. Let $A$ be a subset of a topological space. The closure of $A$, noted $\bar{A}$, is the set of points $x \in X$, such that for each neighborhood $N$ of $x$, $N \cap A \neq \emptyset$.
